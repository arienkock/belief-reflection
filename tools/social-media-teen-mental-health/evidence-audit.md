Marked thin rather than padded; the strongest counter-reading (tiny averages) lives on n_assoc_magnitude and is crosslinked.
- **`n_assoc_benefits`** (one-sided (no AGAINST)). S clusters: NAS-2024; C clusters: —; X clusters: odgers-review. NAS-2024 is the only qualifying FOR cluster; Odgers-review carries the both-directions nuance as COMPLICATES. No credible AGAINST located — flagged thin, not manufactured.
- **`n_trend_real`** (one-sided (no AGAINST)). S clusters: twenge, nordic-trend; C clusters: —; X clusters: nordic-trend, ihme-gbd. The measurement-artifact hypothesis is carried honestly as a COMPLICATES sourced from within a pro-real-increase paper (nordic-trend); a standalone quantitative artifact source did not qualify under the protocol at authoring time. Thin. Geographic (F-geo-3): GBD 2021 (204 countries) reframes the rise as high-income-concentrated (fastest in North America) with global rates broadly stable — the deterioration is a high-income phenomenon in the modelled burden data, not a worldwide one.
- **`n_trend_attrib`** (FOR single-cluster). S clusters: twenge; C clusters: NAS-2024, odgers-review, ihme-gbd; X clusters: —. Both FOR findings are the twenge cluster — the audit treats them as one body of work. Two independent clusters (NAS-2024, odgers-review) push AGAINST. This is the map's most conflation-prone claim and is double-crosslinked to n_assoc_causal. Geographic (F-geo-2): GBD 2021 adds that global adolescent-depression rates were stable 1990-2021 with the rise concentrated in high-income North America (EAPC 1.04 prevalence) while East/South Asia and the Caribbean declined — a globally-diffused exposure with a non-global outcome challenges the principal-driver claim.
- **`n_policy_deact`** (FOR single-cluster). S clusters: allcott-gentzkow; C clusters: antwerp-abstinence-meta, reduction-rct-pool; X clusters: reduction-rct-pool. Counter-signal preserved: independent clusters AGAINST its own claim (antwerp-abstinence-meta, reduction-rct-pool) — evidence against was retained, not pruned. Cluster note: reduction-rct-pool deliberately spans Ferguson 2024 AND its 2025 reanalysis — they analyse the same experiment pool and must not corroborate each other.
- **`n_policy_ban`** (one-sided (no FOR)). S clusters: —; C clusters: aus-ban-eval; X clusters: aus-ban-eval. The FOR slot is deliberately empty — no measured evidence that age bans reduce use existed at authoring time. The single AGAINST cluster (aus-ban-eval) is three months of a leaky rollout; the node is scoped to USE, not mental health, and flagged thin.
- **`n_assoc_magnitude`** (editorial). - **`n_mech_sleep`** (editorial). Two strong meta-analyses, one per direction (carter-sleep-meta between-person; uq-withinperson-meta within-person). The disagreement is the design contrast itself — typed as an assumption-sensitivity crosslink.
- **`n_assoc_causal`** (editorial). Counter-signal preserved: independent clusters AGAINST its own claim (NAS-2024, reduction-rct-pool) — evidence against was retained, not pruned.
