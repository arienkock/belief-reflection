## Notes on flagged nodes
- **`n_measure_reliable`** (one-sided (no FOR)). S clusters: —; C clusters: interlab-vamas; X clusters: standardisation. One-sided (no independent FOR): reproducibility is answered by a large interlaboratory comparison (VAMAS 2025) finding poor between-lab agreement; the pro-reliability side is standardisation-in-progress, not a demonstration of achieved consistency, so it is carried as COMPLICATES rather than counted as FOR. Marked thin, not padded with a fake FOR.
- **`n_health_assoc`** (editorial). Single-study leaf: the FOR direction is one observational study (Marfella 2024, marfella-nejm); the AGAINST is a formal contamination critique of that same study (marfella-critique) plus weight-of-evidence reviews (who) noting no demonstrated population-level effect. Mechanically two-sided but genuinely thin — overridden to thin, not presented as well-evidenced.
- **`n_health_cause`** (editorial). Counter-signal preserved: the toxicology-harm cluster (tox-review) is retained FOR, but both the dose-realism critique (dose-critique) and the weight-of-evidence reviews (sapea) are carried AGAINST; the FOR evidence is high-dose and is caveated as showing harm is possible, not harm at realistic dose.
- **`n_environ_wildlife`** (editorial). Genuinely two-sided across three independent clusters: the effects meta-analysis (foley-meta) FOR; concentration-realism (bucci-rochman) and species-sensitivity risk modelling (koelmans-ssd) AGAINST. Retained on both sides rather than resolved.
- **`n_origin_sources`** (editorial). Two-sided from one authoritative source plus the secondary-dominance point: Boucher & Friot ranks primary sources (FOR) while the secondary fraction that dominates the total is unattributed (CHALLENGES). The claim is scoped to 'the biggest contributors', which is contested at the level of the total.
- **`n_policy_bans`** (editorial). The FOR (microbead-ban) is a measured reduction of the TARGETED source; the AGAINST (boucher-friot) is that the target is ≈2% of primary microplastics — a scope tension, not a dispute about whether the ban worked. Both directions are measured.
